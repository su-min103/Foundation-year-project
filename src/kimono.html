Kimono Dress

Product Description

A traditional Japanese garment known for its wide sleeves and wrap-around design. Originating in the Heian period (794-1185), kimonos were worn by men and women of all social classes as a symbol of traditional Japanese fashion. Made from luxurious fabrics with intricate patterns, kimonos are both comfortable and fashionable, making it suitable for any occasion from formal events to casual outing.
